# Topic Brief

## Raw Topic

中小企業 ESG 導入困難

## Why This Is Still Too Broad

This phrase is a workplace concern, not yet a research question. It must be narrowed by context, unit of analysis, available evidence, and method.

## Clarification

| Layer | Working Answer |
| --- | --- |
| Broad keyword | ESG |
| Practical context | Taiwan small and medium-sized enterprises |
| Stakeholders | managers, sustainability staff, external consultants |
| Possible outcome | adoption barriers, reporting readiness, perceived usefulness |
| Possible method | interview study, document analysis, survey, case comparison |
| Feasible data source | public ESG guidance, interview notes, company documents approved for use |
| Main risk | topic becomes a management essay without a research gap |

## Candidate Research Questions

| Candidate | Why It Might Work | Main Risk |
| --- | --- | --- |
| What barriers do Taiwan SMEs face when preparing ESG-related disclosures? | Connects practice to a bounded context | Needs clear definition of ESG disclosure readiness |
| How do managers interpret the value and burden of ESG adoption? | Fits interview or qualitative case design | May become opinion summary without theory |
| What gap exists between public ESG guidance and SME implementation capacity? | Allows document analysis plus interviews | Needs specific guidance documents and comparison criteria |
